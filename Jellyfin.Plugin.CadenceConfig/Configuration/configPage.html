Deezer playlist sync

Importing a Deezer playlist (via the Cadence client) creates a matching Jellyfin playlist and subscribes it. The "Sync Deezer Playlists" scheduled task then keeps each playlist up to date, adding tracks as they arrive in your library (e.g. after Lidarr fills in a previously-missing artist). It only adds — it never removes tracks.

How often the sync task runs. Default 12. Takes effect after the next Jellyfin restart.
